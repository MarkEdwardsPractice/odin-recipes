Potato Salad

This potato salad recipe is one I have been making since I was in elementary school.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Recipe

  1. Cut Potatoes into small cubes and boil
  2. Boil eggs
  3. Drain the pot with potatoes of water
  4. Peel the eggs
  5. Put the eggs into the pot with potatoes
  6. Add mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper to taste
  7. Use a large spoon or other cooking utensil to crush and mix everything together
