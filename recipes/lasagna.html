Lasagna

This recipe for lasagna is one I came up with and it will be one you will love.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • Diced garlic
  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  1. Blend tomatoes
  2. Fry garlic in a pan until brown
  3. Pour tomato juice in a pan
  4. Add oregano and reduce mixture to a saucy paste
  5. Boil lasagna noodles in a pot with salt
  6. Mix ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and parsley together
  7. Add layers in a baking container in this order:
    1. Tomato Sauce
    2. Cheese Mixture
    3. Noodles
    4. Repeat
  8. When it reaches the top of the pan, finish it off with tomato sauce and then add a top layer of cheese mixture.
  9. Bake at 400 for 30 minutes or until the cheese on top is brown.
