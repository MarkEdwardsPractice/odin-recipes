Ramen

This is a recipe for my favorite ramen in Korea

Ingredients

  • Jin Ramen, Red Package

Recipe

  1. Boil water
  2. Open the Jin Ramen Package and put the noodles and dried vegetables in the boiling water
  3. Boil three minutes
  4. Open the spice package and pour it in
  5. Mix it quickly with the noodles and turn off the gas
